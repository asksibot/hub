Why Asksibot

Comprehensive Occupational Therapy Resources

Asksibot offers an all-encompassing platform for Occupational Therapy, providing expertly curated resources that cater to individuals interested in neurodiversity and sensory challenges. Our tools are designed to equip you with the knowledge and insights necessary for effective understanding of self and others.

Customizable Surveys and Assessments

With Asksibot, users have access to tailored Occupational Therapy surveys and assessments that help identify sensory preferences and working styles. These targeted tools ensure that you gain meaningful insights, which can be saved and downloaded for future reference.

Empowering Neurodiversity and Sensory Awareness

Asksibot is dedicated to fostering an inclusive environment by raising awareness and providing resources for those interested in neurodiversity and sensory challenges. Our platform empowers users with the knowledge and tools needed to embrace and support diverse sensory experiences effectively.

Pictograms and Visual Stories for ASD

We understand the importance of visual aids in communication, especially for individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). Asksibot provides guidance on using pictograms and visual stories to enhance understanding and interaction. Learn how to leverage resources from ARASAAC to support those with ASD effectively.
